Typography Exercise

Client

Date

April 2022

Category

Design

During the Spring of 2022, I found myself fascinated with typography, which began this passion project of creating this font. Though still a work in progress and yet to be named, it is a piece I'm particularly fond of.

Similar to Old English, it draws on Caslon Black: a typeface originally cast by William Caslon in 18th century England that combined the design attributes of both the medieval and Victorian eras. However, I also took inspiration from Several Contemporary tattoo artists in creating this font, mainly Keegan Dakkar. I hope to take on similar typography projects in the near future.
